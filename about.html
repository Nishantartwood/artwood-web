A Legacy of
Design excellence

Founded in 1994 by Mr. Ranjeet Sharma, Art Wood has grown from a visionary venture into a cornerstone of innovation and elegance in interior design. Under his leadership, Art Wood has set benchmarks in creativity and craftsmanship, transforming spaces with a blend of traditional techniques and modern aesthetics. Today, the company stands as a testament to enduring quality and exceptional design.
